## Game Overview
Hexwood Hunt invites players to explore the enigmatic world of Hexwood, a forest shrouded in mystery and tales of old. As a young herbalist seeking a cure for a mysterious illness, players navigate through dense, pixelated woodlands, encounter otherworldly creatures, and unravel the forest's hidden secrets.

## Story
In Hexwood, the protagonist seeks rare herbs but faces an unexpected twist â€“ they are bitten by strange zombie-like animals inhabiting the forest. The bite causes a peculiar illness, and rumors suggest that the cure lies deep within the woods. Armed with courage and determination, the protagonist ventures further into Hexwood, collecting specific plants to stave off the disease and searching for the elusive cure.

  - Immersive Storytelling: Traverse a world rich in lore, where each choice carves a path through the mysteries of Hexwood.
